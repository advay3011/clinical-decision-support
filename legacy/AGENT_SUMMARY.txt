================================================================================
THE 7 TOOLS (What It Can Do)
================================================================================

1. FETCH STOCK DATA
   - Gets 30 days of stock prices
   - Input: Stock symbol (AAPL, GOOGL, TSLA)
   - Output: Daily prices, volume, highs/lows

2. MOVING AVERAGE
   - Checks if stock is going UP or DOWN
   - Compares current price to 7-day average
   - Output: BULLISH (up) or BEARISH (down)

3. VOLATILITY ANALYSIS
   - Measures how risky the stock is
   - Calculates price swings
   - Output: LOW, MEDIUM, or HIGH risk

4. PRICE PREDICTION
   - Predicts where stock will go in 5 days
   - Uses math on past prices
   - Output: Direction (UP/DOWN) + target price

5. SUPPORT & RESISTANCE
   - Finds "floor" (support) and "ceiling" (resistance)
   - Shows where price bounces or gets rejected
   - Output: BUY/SELL/HOLD signal

6. ALIBI ANALYSIS ‚≠ê NEW
   - Explains WHY the price moved
   - Detects: volume surge, news, bounces, etc.
   - Output: 3 possible reasons ranked by confidence

7. TRADING REPORT
   - Summarizes all analysis in one report
   - Combines all 6 tools
   - Output: Professional formatted report
