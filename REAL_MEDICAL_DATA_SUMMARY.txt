NEW TOOLS
================================================================================

1. get_real_drug_info(drug_name)
   - Get real drug information from RxNorm
   - Returns: drug name, RxCUI, type

2. check_real_drug_interactions(drug_names)
   - Check real drug interactions from RxNorm
   - Returns: severity, description, safety status

3. get_drug_adverse_events(drug_name)
   - Get real adverse events from OpenFDA
   - Returns: adverse events, report counts
